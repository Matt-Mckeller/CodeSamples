**Happiness:**
Elara woke to sunlight streaming through her window.  A robin sang outside.  She smiled, remembering the laughter shared with friends yesterday at the park.  The scent of baking bread drifted from the kitchen, a familiar comfort.  Today held no grand plans, just the small joys that filled her heart with a quiet, persistent happiness.
